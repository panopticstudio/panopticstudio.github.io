About Collections

A collection is a subset of our dataset we choose to help researchers to access our dataset more conveniently and coherently. Due to the large data size (hundreds TB), it would not be hard to try to use all of our dataset at the beginning, and for those who are wondering at which sequences they need to start, this "Collection" should be a good recommendation. We expect that this coherent starting point can also make it easier to compare results among approaches.
